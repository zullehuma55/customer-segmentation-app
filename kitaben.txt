kitaben
kitabon ko chat diya maine
socha milegi kamyabi bas issi sey

nahin pata tha mujhe tabhi ke
imtehaan abhi khatam nahin hai

bade imtehaan to abhi baaki they
jinsey main abhi thi anjaan

zamane me kai hain log maujood
kitaabon se kahin door hain unke Karamat

bas kitabon se mili Roshni kaafi nahin
mehnat se usey mashal bananatha baki
